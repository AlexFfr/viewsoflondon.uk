Staple Lane, East Clandon

Beautiful London Vista with views of Wembley Stadium, Central London, Canary Wharf all the way to Crystal Palace Transmitting Tower.

On a clear day the entirity of London is visible, from an impressive distance of 25 miles!

Woking and surrounding valley is also viewable to the North further down the road.
